POSSIBILITIES ARE ENDLESS

AzkDecore was founded in 2022 as a result of a passion for dynamic events and decore. Our goal is to provide unrivaled event services and exceptional customer service.

Why AzkDecore? Because with us, the possibilities are limitless. We are dedicated to creating one-of-a-kind events that your guests will remember for years to come, no matter how big or small. Customization is guaranteed, from your floor plan to your regular menu and signature lounge furniture.

We collaborate with the best vendors in the Addis Abeba area to provide planning and design services at the venue of your dreams, as well as full service at the friendship park Event Spaces.
